I offer the following collaboration opportunities for students and future researchers:

#### Opportunities for Undergraduate Students

**Course and/or Lab Assistants**

I'm looking for undergraduate students who want to participate as assistants in the courses I'll teach this semester. You'll be able to collaborate in both teaching and lab activities, gaining valuable practical and academic experience.

**Undergraduate Thesis**

If you're an undergraduate student looking for a thesis topic, I have innovative projects I can suggest in areas such as Image Segmentation, Activity Recognition, and Object Detection. I'm also open to discussing topics you already have in mind.

**Social Service**

If you need to complete your Social Service, I offer projects where you can collaborate on research and technological development activities. You'll have the opportunity to contribute to ongoing projects while strengthening your technical and analytical skills in areas related to computer vision, artificial intelligence, and data science.

#### Requirements

For undergraduates: interest in research and collaboration in technological areas, with basic programming knowledge.

#### How to Apply

If you're interested in any of these opportunities, email me your CV along with a brief description of your proposal or research interests.

#### Positions

- **Course Assistant**: Focuses on defining problems/exercises to be explained/presented in person and used for grading the course.
- **Lab Assistant**: Focuses on defining programs to be explained/presented in person and used for grading the course.
- **Thesis Student**: Focuses on doing research aimed at publications and an undergraduate thesis.
